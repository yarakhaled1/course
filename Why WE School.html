Why WE School


WHY WE-SCHOOL?

WE School is the first smart governmental school equipped with the highest level of modern technologies and approved curricula to qualify technicians for the labor market to be a development step in the future of Egypt.


The school students are provided with practical training in the fields of communications, programming, electricity and learn other subjects. The school develops the students in the behavioral aspects of their lives on how to make decisions and communicate with others to make them able to simulate the labor market.






We School is a co-ed school and supports the joint work of males and females


The school's goal is to bring out a new generation of technicians in the field of communications and information technology who are able to build an integrated digital society to keep pace with the latest technologies and methods of education in force internationally.



WHAT ARE THE ADVANTAGES?

  1. The most prominent feature of WE School is that the student studies everything related to information technology, communication systems, and electrical systems.


  2. WE School allows the graduates to join and complete their studies in faculties of engineering and technological universities, after they passed the equivalency.


  3. WE School provides a world-class teaching staff, including doctors and engineers specialized in the field.


  4. The sponsor company (WE) also provides job opportunities in the company for the top achievers upon their graduation.
